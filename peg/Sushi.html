Sushi Recipe

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish that combines vinegared rice with various ingredients such as seafood, vegetables, and sometimes tropical fruits. Here's how you can make it at home:

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 ½ cups water
  • ½ cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 sheets nori (seaweed)
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 8 oz sashimi-grade raw fish (such as tuna or salmon), sliced
  • Soy sauce (for serving)
  • Wasabi (for serving)
  • Pickled ginger (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the sushi rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Combine the rice and water in a rice cooker and cook according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  2. In a small saucepan, combine the rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Cook over medium heat until the sugar and salt dissolve. Allow to cool.
  3. Transfer the cooked rice to a large bowl and gently fold in the vinegar mixture. Let the rice cool to room temperature.
  4. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat. Spread a thin layer of sushi rice over the nori, leaving a 1-inch border at the top.
  5. Arrange the cucumber, avocado, and fish slices in a line along the bottom edge of the rice.
  6. Using the bamboo mat, roll the sushi tightly from the bottom to the top, pressing firmly to seal.
  7. Slice the roll into bite-sized pieces using a sharp knife.
  8. Serve the sushi with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
