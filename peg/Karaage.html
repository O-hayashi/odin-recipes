Karaage Recipe

Karaage is a popular Japanese fried chicken dish, known for its juicy marinated chicken coated in a crispy shell. Here's how you can make it at home:

Ingredients

  • 1 pound skin-on chicken thighs, cubed
  • 1 tablespoon sake
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ egg, beaten
  • ⅓ cup potato starch
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chicken, sake, sugar, soy sauce, ginger, and garlic. Mix well and marinate for at least 15 minutes in the fridge.
  2. Add the beaten egg and potato starch to the marinated chicken. Mix well to coat the chicken evenly.
  3. Heat the oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 340°F (170°C).
  4. Fry the chicken pieces in batches until golden brown and fully cooked, about 5-7 minutes per batch.
  5. Drain the fried chicken on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  6. Serve hot with a wedge of lemon and enjoy!
